After you enabled billing access on your AWS account, how can you grant access to billing data to specific IAM users or groups explicitely?

Options

1. You need to create and attach a Customer Managed Policy to the specific user or group
2. You need to wait for the next billing cycle
3. You need to retrieve a Security Token from the billing console and give it to the specific user or group
4. You need to create a Trust between the billing console and the user or group
